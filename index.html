About JZAC

Built in Spokane Valley, made to last outdoors.

JZac Designs started in Spokane Valley, WA in 2024 on a simple idea: small businesses deserve sign work that looks like it cost more than it did. Today that means everything from a single storefront decal to full fleet lettering — plus a growing line of 3D printed custom pieces designed and printed in-house.

Every job starts with a free digital proof before anything gets cut. Vinyl work is cut from premium cast film rated for years of outdoor use, not the bargain stuff that fades and cracks. And in most cases, the person who designed your piece is the same person who installs it.
